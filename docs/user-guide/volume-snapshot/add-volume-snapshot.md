## Add Volume Snapshot in StackBill CMP

- click on the **Virtual Resources** dropdown below the DashBoard icon, which is available in the top left corner. From the dropdown, click on **Snapshot** option.

<img alt="stackbill Cloud billing solution" src="/user-guide/volume-snapshot/add-volume-snapshot/stackbill-cloud-management-portal-snapshot.png" width="80%" />

- Volume Snapshot screen appears.
- Under the **action** option on the right side of the panel, users can see a **Create Volume button**.
- Click on it.

<img alt="stackbill Cloud billing solution" src="/user-guide/volume-snapshot/add-volume-snapshot/stackbill-cloud-management-portal-create-volume.png" width="80%" />

- Create Volume screen appears.

<img alt="stackbill Cloud billing solution" src="/user-guide/volume-snapshot/add-volume-snapshot/stackbill-cloud-management-portal-create-volume-name.png" width="50%" />

- Specify a name for the volume.
- Click **Create** button.

<img alt="stackbill Cloud billing solution" src="/user-guide/volume-snapshot/add-volume-snapshot/stackbill-cloud-management-portal-successfull-volume.png" width="100%" />

-  Now the Volume has been created successfully from the snapshot.
